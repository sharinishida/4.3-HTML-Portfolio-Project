The Best Movies According to Shari

My top 3 movies of all time:

When a Man Loves a Women

Love how the movie shows how true love can withstand life's most challenging trials, all brought to life by Meg Ryan and Andy Garcia's powerful performances.

The Notebook

The Notebook captivates me with its timeless portrayal of love that transcends obstacles and endures through decades, reminding me that true connection can withstand both time and tragedy.

When Harry Met Sally

It perfectly captures the hilarious journey from friendship to romance with dialogue so authentic you recognize your own relationships in every scene.
